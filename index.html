01 about me

In 2015 I moved from California to South Carolina to enroll in the Iron Yard; a code bootcamp where I learned the fundamentals of front end web development, as well as "learning how to learn". I chose front end web development because it suited my creativity better than the more technical backend web development. In fact, my focus on design as well as code is what I take the most pride in, and lead me to my first employment opportunities. My focus when creating for the web, is to approach a site by focusing on 3 major factors

  • square_footDesign

    Does the website standout? I find it paramount to avoid boring web experiences, so finding the right deisgn is crucial for keeping the experience engaging.

  • codeBuild

    Is the website responsive and its it supported by the widest variety of browsers? Compatibility with devices and browsers is a major focus, often leading to mobile first designs.

  • travel_exploreOptimization

    Does the site have good search engine optimization? Aside from including good content, load times & semantic code are just a couple considerations when optimizing a site.
